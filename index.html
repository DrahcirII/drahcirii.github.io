Will you hear the story?...

Really? You will? I'm so happy...

or so you thought...



...it happened so fast, that you just couldn't react.

Once upon a time, you were a king...a cursed king. You were infamous and black luster among others, a real outcast. The sky - always blank and white, nothing had layed in store for you. You were, in a weird sense, alone and misunderstood.


Did you feel the madness building up on the inside? Some of them did. The cold and emptiness had forced you to leave home to go to a far away land.


You were a stranger in a strange realm. You only knew few of the locals, and still you were alone, with nothing, but your petty spells. Time would pass by and you would meet many kind and generous souls in the new kingdom, one which happened to be the queen of the land.


It was love at first sight...


...the feeling of warmth whenever she stepped into the room, the constant agony of her clawing away at your heartstrings...


...was enough to keep you going, until you needed more. You ask for her hand and the both of you are happy...

or so you thought...

The Requiem Project

Requiem is a concept concieved as a site to promote creativity and free will to the public. The idea is to add change to a society of the few by not only augmenting the thinking of the individual, but by encouraging the spread of ideas. The mission as a group of free thinkers that sail the international waters of the net, is finding the true meaning of freedom through design and self-expression. We liberate thus burying old traditions and norms to usher forth a new generation.

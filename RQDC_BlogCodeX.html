About

Hello everyone^^ I know it's a slight mouthfull, but I'm pretty much on a username basis, so you can call me DRC for short ^_~.


‒‒‒‒‒†‒‒‒‒‒

It is pretty fair that I tell a little more about myself, so...


  • I really love art ^_^! I'd kinda die without it...X_X
  • I also love Anime and Manga^^ (but I watch only the most advertised stuffs! I'm a fan of Eureka 7 and Death Note >:3)
  • My real name is Richard (yeah....cue the roses and short gust of wind behind my cape...)
  • My favorite two foods are rice and pizza (weird, I know... like some sheltered teenager er something... ¬_¬)!
  • I draw almost anything that is asked of me, especially if asked by a close friend
  • I do art of various types and some photography (I deal in working with pencils and/or software to make my pictures)
  • I hope to get to practice more
  • Me and my laptop are like married with how much I work with it <3 <3 <3!
  • I'm overactive when using emoticons...pshhh I love those things ;]
  • I enjoy web design almost as much as I love art!

...And Now...


‒‒‒‒‒†‒‒‒‒‒

Requiem began as my second ever webpage project! So far I've been practicing more advanced code and I plan to take this site very far. I was born in Lansing, MI and I reside in the grand old Motor City. Some time ago I took classes at Grand Circus Co. to polish my web design skills and now I'm an indie developer who seeks to find cool ways to make mysterious and dark style websites. Requiem is going to be my future design company website so hopefully you'll see more of me!

The Requiem Project

Requiem is a concept concieved as a futre group to promote creativity and free will to the public. The idea is to add change to a society of the few by not only augmenting the thinking of the individual, but by encouraging the spread of ideas. The mission as a group of free thinkers that sail the international waters of the net, is finding the true meaning of freedom through design and self-expression. We liberate thus burying old traditions and norms to usher forth a new generation.
